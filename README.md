# AI Usage and Student Academic Performance Analysis

## Objective

Investigate the relationship between AI usage, anxiety levels, and academic performance among students.

## Dataset

Kaggle: AI Usage and Student Academic Performance Analysis

## Questions

1. Which year of study has the highest AI dependency?
2. Are AI users more anxious than non-AI users?
3. Does anxiety increase with AI dependency?
4. How does AI usage relate to GPA?

## Key Findings

- Sophomore students showed the highest AI dependency.
- Anxiety increased steadily with AI dependency.
- Students with higher AI dependency had higher average anxiety scores.
- GPA improvements were observed across all dependency levels.
